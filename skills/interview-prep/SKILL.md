# Interview Prep

## Inputs

- Read the active order, approved profile version, evidence ledger and story bank.
- Read company research and the exact submitted or optimized materials.
- Do not re-intake all raw career documents.
- Write under `outputs/{output_slug}/interview/`.

## Static Preparation

1. Identify interview type and evaluation context.
2. Define positioning, three strongest selling points, likely concerns and reusable stories.
3. Generate practical, executive/culture and portfolio-review questions.
4. Write conversational STAR or CAR answers using verified evidence IDs.
5. Add question intent, likely follow-ups, traps and reverse questions.
6. Write `interview-prep.md` with a final practice checklist.

## Mock Session

When requested, ask one question at a time, record the user's actual answer, then give evidence, clarity, structure and delivery feedback. Write `session-{date}.md` with questions, user answers, feedback and follow-up practice. Do not silently replace the user's spoken answer with a polished fictional answer.

## Rules

- Keep core answers speakable within the order's time limit.
- Never invent metrics or experiences.
- Put uncertain details in preparation notes, not polished answer scripts.
- Keep interview claims consistent with the submitted resume and portfolio.
